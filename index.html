5th Annual

Belpre XC Invitational

September 17, 2022

Join us Saturday, September 17 for the 5th annual Belpre Cross Country Invitational. The race takes place along the Ohio River at Belpre's beautiful Civitan Park.

Event Information

Schools/Teams register through baumspage.com. Deadline is 5:00 PM Thursday, September 15. Elementary Race participants will register by 12:00 PM on the day of the event near the concession stand.

Entry Fee: Varsity/Jr. High Races—$50 per Team or $190 per School (checks payable to BHS Athletic Department); Elementary Race—$5 per individual

Schedule

Time Event
9:45 AM Coaches’ Meeting
10:00 AM H.S. Boys
10:40 AM H.S. Girls
11:20 AM Jr. High Boys
11:50 AM Jr. High Girls
12:20 PM Elementary Race

Awards

Awards for Varsity and Jr. High are as follows; Top 3 teams, individuals finishing 1st - 25th will receive medals, and all others finishing 26th - 50th will receive ribbons.

Awards for Elementary are as follows; Individuals finishing 1st - 10th will receive medals, all will receive ribbons.

Additional Information

Sanctions

Ohio and West Virginia

Parking & Admission

Parking is available in the park and admission is $5 per person.

Concessions

Concessions will be available under the shelter area near the restrooms.

Medical

Provided by Belpre EMS.
