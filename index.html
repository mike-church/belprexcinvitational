Third Annual

Belpre XC Invitational

September 17 and 19, 2020

Join us Thursday, September 17 for the Junior High race and Saturday, September 19 for the High School race. The race will take place along the Ohio River at Belpre's beautiful Civitan Park.

Awards

Awards for Varsity and Jr. High are as follows; Top 3 teams, individuals finishing 1st - 25th will receive medals, and all others finishing 26th - 50th will receive ribbons. Awards will be mailed.

Parking is available in the park and admission is $5 per person.

Concessions will be limited to drinks only and located under the shelter area.
