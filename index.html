Second Annual

Belpre XC Invitational

Saturday, September 14, 2019

Join us for Belpre High School's second annual Cross Country Invitational, Saturday, September 14, 2019, along the Ohio River at Belpre's beautiful Civitan Park.

Event Information

Varsity and Jr. High School teams may register at baumspage.com. The deadline for registration is 5:00 PM EST, Thursday, September 12, 2019. Elementary race participants will register on the day of the event by 12:00 PM near the finish line.

Entery fees for the event will be; $40 per Varsity/Jr. High team or $150 per school. The Elementary race will be $5 per individual.

Awards

Awards for Varsity and Jr. High are as follows; Top 3 teams, individuals finishing 1st - 25th will receive medals, and all others finishing 26th - 50th will receive ribbons. Elementary participants finishing 1st - 10th will receive medals and all others racers will receive ribbons.
